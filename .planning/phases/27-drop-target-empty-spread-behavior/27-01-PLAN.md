<objective>
Two independent className-only visual fixes — both surgical, no behavior changes beyond what the requirements specify.

Purpose:
- CTRL-06: Eliminate misleading drop-target feedback. Currently every drag highlights every opponent's hand zone with a dashed amber border and expands the zone, even when the cursor is nowhere near it. The outline should appear only when the dragged card is physically hovering the zone.
- LAYOUT-06: Make empty personal spread zones discoverable but unobtrusive. Currently they collapse to a 1px invisible line (`h-px opacity-0`), which hides them entirely. Replace with a faint dashed strip at ~¼ normal height so players know the zone exists.

Output: OpponentHand.tsx and SpreadZone.tsx with className-only changes; two source-contract test files asserting the new patterns.
</objective>
