## Interaction Contract

### BUG-01: Pile card selection ring

**Trigger:** User clicks "Select All" on a `PileZone`.

**Before fix:** Top card is selected in state but renders without visual feedback — no ring, no lift.

**After fix:** Top card renders with `ring-1 ring-primary/30 ring-offset-1 ring-offset-background rounded-md transition-transform duration-150` and `translateY(-6px)` lift — identical to the selected state in `SortableSpreadCard` (SpreadZone.tsx:26, 52).

**Prop path:** `BoardDragLayer.selectedIds` → `BoardView` (new prop at call site line 76) → `PileZone` (new `selectedIds` prop) → `DraggableCard` (new `isSelected?: boolean` prop).

**Reference implementation:** `SortableSpreadCard` in `src/components/SpreadZone.tsx` lines 26 and 52.

### BUG-02: Communal grid column collapse

**Before fix:** `GridZone` renders `grid-cols-7` at all viewport widths.

**After fix:** `grid-cols-4 sm:grid-cols-7` — 4 columns below 640px, 7 columns at 640px and above. 14 cells wrap to 4 rows on mobile; this is accepted per the out-of-scope decision in REQUIREMENTS.md.

**Change location:** `src/components/GridZone.tsx` line 148.

**No change to:** outer `overflow-x-auto` wrapper.

Source: CONTEXT.md D-05, D-06, D-07.
