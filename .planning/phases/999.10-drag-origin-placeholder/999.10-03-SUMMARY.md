## What Was Built

Added a `customCollision` function to `BoardDragLayer.tsx` that replaces the global `closestCenter` collision algorithm on `DndContext`. The function:

1. Splits droppable containers into two groups: zone-level (`'hand'` and `'opponent-hand-*'`) vs. everything else (piles, sortable card IDs).
2. Applies `pointerWithin` to the zone group â€” a hit only registers when the pointer coordinate is physically inside the rendered rect.
3. Applies `closestCenter` to the non-zone group â€” pile drops and hand-to-hand sortable reordering behave exactly as before.
4. Returns zone collisions if any exist, otherwise returns the closest-center results.

This directly fixes the root cause of UAT test 3: `HandZone` attaches its droppable ref to a full-width 128px strip container, and `closestCenter` was registering a hand-drop hit as soon as the dragged card's center was nearest the container center â€” even when the pointer was far above the visible strip. With `pointerWithin`, the drop only fires when the pointer is actually inside the 128px strip boundary.
