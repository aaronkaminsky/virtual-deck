## Tests

### 1. Pile card drag placeholder
expected: Drag a card from a pile on the board. While dragging, a dashed card-shaped outline (80×112px, rounded corners) should appear at the card's origin slot — the space where the card was before you picked it up. The outline stays visible for the entire drag.
result: pass

### 2. Hand cross-zone drag placeholder
expected: Drag a card from your hand toward a pile or another zone. While the card is being dragged across zone boundaries, the dashed outline should remain visible at the card's original position in the hand strip for the entire drag — it should not disappear or collapse regardless of where on the screen you drag.
result: pass

### 3. Missed hand-drop snap-back
expected: Drag a hand card past the leftmost card and release over the empty hand strip (no card underneath cursor). The card should snap back to its original position with an animation — not instant vanish. The dashed placeholder should remain visible at the origin slot throughout the snap-back animation.
result: issue
reported: "Drop zones are too big. Can't trigger a missed hand drop because the hand drop zone extends too far into the play area. Similarly, pile and opponent hand drop zones extend too far beyond their visual outlines. Drop should only register if the card is at least partially (or mostly) over the visual boundary."
severity: major

### 4. Hand-to-hand reorder placeholder
expected: Drag a card within your own hand to reorder it (drop directly onto another card). The dashed outline holds the card's original slot while adjacent cards shift to show the drop target. After a successful reorder, the overlay clears immediately (no snap-back).
result: pass

### 5. Drag cancel snap-back
expected: Start dragging a card (from pile or hand), then cancel by releasing outside a valid drop zone or pressing Escape. During the snap-back animation, the dashed placeholder should remain visible at the origin until the card returns to its original position.
result: pass
