<task type="checkpoint:human-verify" gate="blocking">
  <name>Task 2: Verify all Phase 4 game controls</name>
  <what-built>Complete game controls: card flip, pass card, deal, shuffle pile, reset table, and undo.</what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    Open 2-3 browser tabs to the same room URL (e.g., http://localhost:5173?room=test1).

    **1. CARD-03 — Flip card (click pile card):**
    - Click on a card in any pile zone. Verify it toggles between face-up and face-down.
    - Verify the flip is visible in all tabs simultaneously.
    - Verify clicking during/after a short drag does NOT trigger a flip.

    **2. CTRL-02 — Shuffle pile:**
    - Set a pile to face-up (use existing face toggle).
    - Click the "Shuffle" button on that pile.
    - Verify the card order changes visibly in all tabs.

    **3. CTRL-01 — Deal N cards:**
    - Verify the "Deal" button is visible in the controls bar (top-right).
    - Click "Deal" to open the popover. Enter a number (e.g., 5). Click "Deal".
    - Verify each player tab shows the correct number of cards in their hand.
    - Verify the Deal button disappears and Undo + Reset buttons appear.

    **4. CARD-04 — Pass card to opponent:**
    - In one tab, drag a card from your hand onto an opponent's hand zone in the top strip.
    - Verify the recipient tab shows the new card in their hand.
    - Verify the third tab does NOT see the card face (only sees opponent hand count change).

    **5. CTRL-04 — Undo:**
    - After any card action, click the "Undo" button.
    - Verify the previous state is restored in all tabs.
    - Verify the Undo button becomes disabled after use.

    **6. CTRL-03 — Reset table:**
    - Click the "Reset" button. Verify a confirmation dialog appears with "Reset table?" title.
    - Click "Keep playing" — verify nothing changes.
    - Click "Reset" again, then click "Reset table" in the dialog.
    - Verify all cards return to the draw pile, all hands are empty, and the Deal button reappears.
  </how-to-verify>
  <resume-signal>Type "approved" if all 6 controls work correctly, or describe any issues found.</resume-signal>
</task>
