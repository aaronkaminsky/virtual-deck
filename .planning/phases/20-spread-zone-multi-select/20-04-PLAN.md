    Plans 01–03 implemented spread zone multi-select:
    - SortableSpreadCard: selection ring, lift transform, click toggle, aria-pressed
    - SpreadZone: interactive prop (false for opponent zones), "N selected" badge
    - BoardDragLayer: selectionSource state, zone-exclusive selection clearing, extended PLAY_CARD_SET dispatch
    - BoardView: prop threading, interactive={false} for opponent personal zones
    - HandZone: updated onToggleSelect call site
    - party/index.ts: PLAY_CARD_SET branches on fromZone for pile-source moves, handles toZone:'hand'
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    Start the local dev stack as instructed in Task 1 (two browser windows, both in the same room).

    **Test 1 — SPREAD-01: Single card selection**
    1. In window 1: click any card in your personal spread zone (bottom personal zone)
    2. Expected: card lifts up with an amber selection ring
    3. Click the same card again
    4. Expected: ring and lift disappear (deselected)

    **Test 2 — SPREAD-01: Multi-card selection and badge**
    1. In window 1: click 3 different cards in a spread zone
    2. Expected: all 3 show ring + lift; zone header shows "3 selected" badge
    3. Click one of the selected cards
    4. Expected: that card deselects; badge shows "2 selected"

    **Test 3 — SPREAD-01: Escape clears selection**
    1. Select 2+ cards in a spread zone
    2. Press the Escape key
    3. Expected: all rings and lifts disappear; badge disappears

    **Test 4 — SPREAD-01: Zone-exclusive selection**
    1. Select 2 cards in your personal spread zone
    2. Click a card in the communal zone (or in your hand)
    3. Expected: personal spread zone selection clears; new card in the communal zone (or hand) becomes selected

    **Test 5 — SPREAD-03: Multi-card drag to pile**
    1. Select 2+ cards in a spread zone
    2. Drag one of the selected cards onto a pile zone (draw or discard pile)
    3. Expected: all selected cards move to the pile; selection clears

    **Test 6 — SPREAD-03: Multi-card drag to hand**
    1. Select 2+ cards in the communal spread zone
    2. Drag one of the selected cards to your hand zone (bottom strip)
    3. Expected: all selected cards appear in your hand; selection clears

    **Test 7 — SPREAD-03: Multi-card drag to another spread zone**
    1. Select 2+ cards in your personal spread zone
    2. Drag one of the selected cards onto the communal spread zone
    3. Expected: all selected cards appear in the communal zone; selection clears

    **Test 8 — D-07: Opponent zone is drop-only**
    1. In window 2 (player 2): deal some cards to spread zones
    2. In window 1 (player 1): attempt to drag a card from player 2's personal spread zone
    3. Expected: no drag ghost appears; cards in player 2's zone are not draggable

    **Test 9 — D-03: Badge only shows for the owning zone**
    1. Select 3 cards in your personal spread zone
    2. Verify: only your personal spread zone header shows "3 selected"; communal zone header shows no badge
  </how-to-verify>
