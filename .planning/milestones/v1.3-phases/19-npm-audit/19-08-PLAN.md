<objective>
Close Gap 8: the hamburger button (ControlsBar) is vertically centered in the tall opponent header instead of being top-aligned.

Root cause: the header div uses `flex items-center justify-between`. The opponents strip (card backs + spread zones) is tall — often 200px+. With `items-center`, the ControlsBar wrapper is centered in that height, pushing the hamburger to the middle of the header.

Fix: add `self-start` to the ControlsBar wrapper `<div>`. `self-start` overrides the parent's `items-center` for this specific child, pinning the hamburger to the top-right corner of the header.

This is a one-token, one-file, one-line fix.
</objective>
