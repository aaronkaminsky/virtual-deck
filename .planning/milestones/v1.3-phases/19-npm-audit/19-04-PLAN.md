        </Button>
      </div>

Why this exact change:
- `h-7 w-7 p-0` → 28x28px square button (was ~110px wide with text). Three pile columns now fit in 375px with comfortable margin.
- `Eye` (faceUp=true) and `EyeOff` (faceUp=false) are the standard lucide-react semantic for "visible" / "hidden" — direct read for the face-up/face-down concept.
- `aria-label` added (the existing `title` covers hover; `aria-label` covers screen readers since visible text is removed). Both are required for the gap closure: removing visible text without adding an accessible name would degrade a11y.
- The `Shuffle className="w-3 h-3 mr-1"` becomes `Shuffle className="w-4 h-4"` — no `mr-1` because there's no trailing text to space against.
