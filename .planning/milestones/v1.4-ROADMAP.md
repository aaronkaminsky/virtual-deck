# Milestone v1.4: Table Polish

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-05-18
**Phases:** 22–25
**Total Plans:** 10

## Overview

Table Polish adds hand management improvements, bulk-select affordances, a structured play area grid, and compact visual layout to the board. Players can now reveal their hand to the table, sort cards by suit or rank, select entire piles or spread zones for group moves, play cards into a 2-row grid, and enjoy a cleaner board with empty zones that show only their label.

## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- `handRevealed` server-owned: prevents clients from forging revealed state; viewFor masking keeps the privacy invariant intact for revealed hands
- `skipSnapshot?: boolean` on REORDER_HAND: sort is a display preference, not a game move — excluding it from undo stack matches player expectations
- Select All on pile selects top card only: interior cards are masked client-side (client has no card IDs for them); PLAY_CARD_SET would reject them
- MOVE_GRID_CARD as separate action: keeps personal spread zones unaffected; grid and spread are semantically distinct
- Grid cell IDs prefixed `grid-cell-`: allows pointerWithin collision detection to route intra-grid drags independently from pile drops
- `SpreadZone` hidden when empty with drag-reveal: personal zones claiming vertical space when unused was the biggest waste of board area
