  <how-to-verify>
    Setup (run in two terminals):
    1. Terminal A: `npm run dev` (starts PartyKit at http://localhost:1999)
    2. Terminal B: `npm run dev:client` (starts Vite at http://localhost:5173)
    3. Open http://localhost:5173 in a browser. Create a room and join.
    4. Deal cards to yourself so you have a few cards in hand. Drag at least two cards from your hand onto the canvas in roughly the middle of the play area.

    OVERLAP-01 — hit-testing (D-01):
    5. Place two canvas cards so they overlap by roughly 40–50% (one obviously on top of the other; lower-z card's edge clearly visible).
    6. Click directly on the overlapping region (the part where both cards' bodies are stacked). The TOP card (higher z) must be the one that starts to drag when you press-and-drag from that point. The bottom card must NOT activate.
    7. Click on the EXPOSED EDGE of the bottom (lower-z) card — the strip that is not under the top card. The BOTTOM card must drag from that interaction.

    OVERLAP-02 — drag opacity (D-08, Phase 32 D-13):
    8. Drag any canvas card. While the pointer is held down and the card is in flight, the dragged card ghost must look semi-transparent (about 50% opacity) so cards underneath it are partially visible.

    OVERLAP-03 — static shadow (D-02, D-03, D-04, Plan 01):
    9. Drop two cards into a position where the top card covers MORE than 50% of the lower card. The TOP card must show a visible drop shadow at rest (white + gray paper-edge effect; matches STACK_SHADOW = '2px 2px 0 0 #fff, 2px 2px 0 1px #d1d5db'). The bottom card must NOT have the shadow.
    10. Slide the top card slowly until it covers LESS than 50% of the lower card. The shadow on the top card must disappear when the overlap drops below the threshold.

    OVERLAP-03 — drag-time shadow (D-05, D-06, Plan 02):
    11. Pick up a canvas card and slowly drag it OVER another canvas card. While the dragged card's current screen position covers more than 50% of any other canvas card, the dragged ghost must ALSO show the same stack shadow. When you move it away (covering <50% of any other card), the shadow on the ghost must disappear. The transition should be crisp — the shadow appears/disappears as you cross the threshold, not continuously flickering.

    Regression check (Phase 32 behaviors preserved):
    12. Drag a card from the canvas back to your hand — must work as before. Drag a card from the canvas to the draw pile — must work as before. Press Escape mid-drag on a canvas card — card must return to its original position. Drag a hand card onto the canvas — must place at drop point.

    Report what you observed for each numbered step. If any step fails, describe what you saw vs. what was expected.
  </how-to-verify>
