After GAP-02 fix:
- The populated (non-empty) branch — the `'min-w-[56px] h-[64px] sm:min-w-[80px] sm:h-[88px] rounded-lg border flex items-center px-2 overflow-x-auto bg-secondary'` string — gains `py-2`.
- The empty + isOver variant — `'min-w-[56px] sm:min-w-[80px] h-[40px] sm:h-[56px] border border-dashed border-primary rounded-lg flex items-center px-2'` — gains `py-2` as well to keep the over-state slot in visual proportion when a card hovers a previously-empty zone.
- The empty + not-isOver variant — `'h-4 border border-dashed border-muted-foreground/30 rounded-md'` — stays unchanged. It is a 4px-tall strip with no lighter background; it has no visible padding to be asymmetric.

Constraint: the fixed heights `h-[64px] sm:h-[88px]` (and the over-state `h-[40px] sm:h-[56px]`) are RETAINED. The existing card content uses `flex items-center` and intrinsic card heights (cards are 56-88px tall). Adding `py-2` inside a fixed-height container will shrink the available content area by 16px (8px top + 8px bottom). Card height (88px on sm+) exceeds the resulting content height (88 - 16 = 72px), so the card will visually overflow the padding — that is acceptable because the inner content uses `overflow-x-auto` on the populated branch; vertical overflow is not clipped (no overflow-y rule) and the card retains its full visible size while the lighter bg-secondary slot extends 8px above and below the card edge. Visually this gives the lighter background a 4-corner pad rather than a left/right-only pad. Confirm against UI-SPEC.md §"Spacing Scale" — `sm` token is 8px which is exactly `py-2`.

PileZone.tsx slot is NOT modified in this plan. Its slot is sized exactly to the card (no slack), and its `bg-secondary` slot is the card-shaped slot itself; the operator's gap callout focused on the lighter-background BEHIND the slot (the spread-zone strip). If during execution the executor confirms that PileZone has the same visual asymmetry, raise it as a follow-up — do not modify in this plan without verifying.
